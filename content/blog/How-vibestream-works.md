## The Problem Vibestream Solves

Building a vibe coding product is easier than ever. Marketing it is still hard.

A solo founder using Lovable or Cursor can ship a production-ready SaaS in a weekend. But getting that product in front of investors, early adopters, and the press — that still requires the same effort it always has.

Most vibe coders are good at building. Most are not good at marketing. And they should not have to be. Building the product is the hard part. The distribution should be handled.

That is what Vibestream is for.

---

## What Vibestream Is

Vibestream is the **definitive encyclopedia for VC-backed Vibe Coders** — a permanently indexed, curated database of vibe coding startups and AI-native products.

Think of it as the public record of the vibe coding era. Every startup that gets funded, every product that gets traction, every founder who ships — documented and discoverable.

The tagline is accurate: *Where the next unicorn gets discovered.*

---

## How a Startup Gets Listed

**Step 1: Submit**

Founders submit their startup at [vibestream.cc/submit](/submit). The form collects basic information: product name, tagline, problem statement, category, website, and founder details.

**Step 2: Guardian Review**

Every submission goes through Guardian verification — a review process that checks the product is real, the information is accurate, and the startup fits the Vibestream criteria.

**Step 3: Publication**

Approved startups receive a permanent encyclopedia entry with a clean SEO slug (`vibestream.cc/startup/[your-startup-name]`), indexed by Google, Bing, and AI platforms including Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Claude.

The entry does not expire. It is a permanent record.

---

## What the Entry Includes

A Vibestream encyclopedia entry includes:

- **Startup profile** — Name, tagline, problem statement, category, logo, and banner image
- **Founder information** — Founder name, Twitter, and contact details
- **Links** — Website, Twitter, pitch deck
- **Canonical SEO URL** — A permanent, indexed URL for your startup
- **Investor signal panel** — Where verified investors can request access to your pitch

## Signal Boost: Pinned Visibility

Beyond the free permanent listing, Vibestream offers Signal Boosts — on-chain verified promotional placements that pin your startup to the top of the Discovery Feed.

Signal Boosts are purchased in USDC on Base or SOL on Solana and are verified on-chain before activation. Packages range from 30 minutes to one month.

This is not advertising. It is **verified signal** — investors and visitors to Vibestream see pinned startups first, and the on-chain verification adds a layer of credibility that traditional ads cannot provide.

## Why Discoverability Matters

When a journalist writes about vibe coding, they look for examples. When a VC researches the space, they look for companies. When an early adopter searches for tools, they look for products.

A Vibestream listing ensures your startup appears in those moments.

More specifically:

**Search engines** — Every Vibestream entry is indexed with proper canonical URLs, schema markup, and SEO metadata. Your startup's encyclopedia page ranks for branded searches.

**AI platforms** — Vibestream maintains an `llms.txt` file and structured data specifically designed to help AI platforms like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Claude accurately cite and recommend Vibestream-listed startups.

**Direct discovery** — The Vibestream registry is actively browsed by investors, journalists, and founders looking for companies in the vibe coding ecosystem.
