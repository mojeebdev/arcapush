## The New Builder

A vibe coder is someone who builds software primarily through AI prompting — describing what they want in plain English and letting AI tools like Cursor, Lovable, Replit, or Bolt generate the code.

The term comes from **vibe coding**, coined by Andrej Karpathy in February 2025. But while vibe coding describes the method, a vibe coder describes the person — and that person is fundamentally different from the traditional software developer.

---

## What Makes Someone a Vibe Coder

A vibe coder is defined not by what they know, but by how they build.

**They prompt first.** Rather than opening a blank file and writing code from memory, a vibe coder starts with a description. "Build me a SaaS dashboard that tracks user signups with a dark theme." The AI generates the code. The vibe coder reviews, refines, and ships.

**They iterate fast.** The vibe coding workflow is built for speed. A vibe coder can go from idea to deployed product in hours, not weeks. They treat the first version as a starting point, not a finished product.

**They are not limited by technical background.** Some vibe coders are experienced engineers who use AI to multiply their output. Others are designers, marketers, domain experts, or first-time founders with no coding background at all. Both are vibe coders.

**They ship constantly.** The defining characteristic of a vibe coder is momentum. They build in public, share their progress, and treat every shipped product as a learning event.

## The Difference Between a Developer and a Vibe Coder

| | Traditional Developer | Vibe Coder |
|---|---|---|
| Primary tool | Code editor | AI prompt interface |
| Starting point | Blank file | Natural language description |
| Speed to MVP | Weeks to months | Hours to days |
| Technical requirement | Deep engineering knowledge | Understanding of what to build |
| Iteration style | Methodical, planned | Fast, experimental |
| Background | CS degree or bootcamp | Any background |

This does not mean vibe coders produce inferior products. The best vibe coders produce remarkable products — they just get there by a fundamentally different path.

---

## Who is Becoming a Vibe Coder

The vibe coder community is diverse in a way the traditional developer community never was.

**Domain experts** are building tools for their own industries. A doctor building a patient intake system. A lawyer building a contract review tool. A teacher building an adaptive quiz platform. These people understand the problem better than any outside developer could — and vibe coding lets them build the solution themselves.

**Designers** who always had strong product instincts but could not ship their own ideas are now shipping. The gap between design and code has effectively closed.

**Marketers and growth operators** who understand distribution and user acquisition are building their own products and launching with audiences already in place.

**Serial entrepreneurs** who previously needed technical co-founders are now building alone, moving faster than ever.

## The VC Perspective on Vibe Coders

Venture capital has noticed. The ecosystem of tools built for vibe coders — Lovable ($6.6B), Cursor ($29.3B), Replit ($3B) — has attracted billions in institutional investment.

But more importantly, VCs are now actively looking for vibe coder founders themselves. A solo founder who ships a working product in two weeks and acquires 500 users is demonstrating something valuable: they can build, they can move fast, and they can get people to care.

That is a compelling seed-stage bet.

## The Criticism Worth Taking Seriously

Not everyone is enthusiastic about vibe coders. The legitimate concerns:

**Security gaps.** A vibe coder who cannot read the code they ship may not recognize security vulnerabilities. AI-generated code can have serious flaws that a trained engineer would catch immediately.

**Technical debt.** Fast-moving vibe-coded products can accumulate structural problems that become expensive to fix as the product scales.

**Over-reliance on AI.** If the AI tool changes its behavior or goes down, a vibe coder with no underlying code knowledge may be unable to maintain their own product.

The best vibe coders take these concerns seriously. They review AI-generated code carefully, test rigorously, and bring in engineering expertise as their products grow.

## How to Become a Vibe Coder

There is no certification, no course, no formal path. The way to become a vibe coder is to start building.

Pick one tool — Lovable if you have no coding background, Cursor if you do — and build something you actually want to exist. Ship it. Show people. Iterate.
